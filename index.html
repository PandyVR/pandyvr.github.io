About Project Games V3

Project Games V3 is a fast-paced, movement-focused fan game inspired by Gorilla Tag — delivering smooth physics, expressive cosmetics, and chaotic, high-energy tag-style gameplay. Whether you're racing friends, exploring hidden secrets, or mastering advanced parkour routes, Project Games V3 brings nonstop action and replayability.

Features

Fluid Movement & Physics

Swing, climb, bounce, and launch yourself with physics-based movement that feels intuitive and immersive.

Multiple Maps & Environments

Explore a variety of handcrafted maps — from dense jungles to tricky obstacle courses — each offering unique challenges and secrets.

Gamemodes for All Styles

Speed-run races, free roam, hide-and-seek, or custom community modes. There’s something for everyone.

Customizable Cosmetics

Express yourself with skins, accessories, and more — stand out as a skillful, stylish gorilla in the crowd.
